I use Picktime to handle all my scheduling and payment information.

To register, click the button below, click the type of tutoring you'd like, select my name, then select a time. If you don't see any times available, try clicking on different days of the calendar.

Once you pick a time, you will be prompted to input your contact information. You can then pay using Paypal. Once your payment is complete, you should see an appointment confirmation page with a zoom link. The same confirmation will also be emailed to you.

Please email me if you face any issues.
